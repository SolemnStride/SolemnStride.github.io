About Us

Dr. Arnold Rugama graduated from the University of California at San Diego School of Medicine in 1995 and later earned his Diplomate in Family Medicine. He developed a passion for Sleep Medicine and later earned his Diplomate in Sleep Medicine in 2005. He currently practices Sleep Medicine full time at Valley Regional Sleep Disorders Center in Fresno, California, one of the few accredited centers in the California central valley. Dr. Rugama has been using CPAP for over 10 years and regularly samples all the new technologies related to sleep apnea therapy.

William (Bill) Cribbs completed his computer engineering (EECS) degree at California Polytechnic University, San Luis Obispo, California in 2003. For the last decade he has developed new technology for global emerging markets. In addition to developing software computing applications for many of the major broadcasters in the US, Europe, and Australia he has, most recently, contributed to media processing applications for digital cinema using embedded technology. His professional interests include high-end audio and video processing technologies, embedded development, proto-typing, software/hardware security and software architecture. After 7 years with Harris Corporation, Bill Cribbs moved to Morro Bay in 2010, to pursue his passion for nocturnal telemetry systems, working with Dr. Arnold Rugama as well as continue his development with embedded systems for high-quality audio and video systems as a software engineer at Ultra Stereo Labs in San Luis Obispo. Bill Cribbs is married and has one daughter, his personal interests include exploring the central coast, camping and fly fishing.
