Patient Responses

Patient responses upon awakening: There are 5 basic questions that are commonly asked to assess patient satisfaction with their PAP experience. Two questions address and compare the patient's sleep continuity and overall alertness, two questions address mask comfort and effectiveness, and one question asks about comfort with the PAP device pressure settings.

Questions

  • I. Do You Feel Refreshed?
    • 1. Refreshed
    • 2. Could be more Refreshed
    • 3. Not refreshed
  • II. Did you wake up?
    • 1. (0) times
    • 2. (1-3) times
    • 3. (3+) times
  • III. Was the mask Comfortable?
    • 1. Mask was comfortable
    • 2. Mask was not comfortable
  • IV. Did you experience Mask Leaks?
    • 1. No Mask Leaks
    • 2. Mask Leaks
  • V. Was the pressure comfortable?
    • 1. Comfortable
    • 2. Too High
    • 3. Too Low
